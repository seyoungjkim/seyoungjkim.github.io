I’m Seyoung Kim, a comedy writer and performer based in Brooklyn, NY.

Comedy

I write and perform sketch with the weekend sketch team Milwaukee at the Magnet Theater and indie sketch team Queer Window at venues including the Upright Citizen’s Brigade, Brooklyn Comedy Collective, Caveat, and more. I also co-wrote, directed, and produced “A Sketch Show… Or Else” with my comedy partner Jacob Stone. My work has been performed at festivals include SF Sketchfest, Austin Sketch Fest, DC Sketchfest, Philly Sketchfest, and PackFest.

I host the monthly show Sketch Cage Match at UCB!

You can also see me perform improv comedy with indie teams OOF! and The Slaysians.

But if you prefer to watch comedy from a place of disembodiment, I create digital sketches with my group sad sacks.
