About

I’m Seyoung Kim, a comedy writer and performer based in Brooklyn, NY.

Comedy

I write sketch for Party Trick, a house team at the Magnet Theater. The team has been selected for festivals including Austin Sketch Fest, DC Sketchfest, Philly Sketchfest, and PackFest.

I’ve also directed sketch for Magnet’s REMIX Diversity Sketch Lab.

Most recently, I co-wrote, directed, and produced “A Sketch Show… Or Else” with my comedy partner Jacob Stone.

And finally, you can see me perform improv comedy with my indie team Paid 2 Be Here.
